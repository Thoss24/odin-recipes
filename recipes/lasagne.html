Lasagne recipe


Description

Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings such as rag√π (ground meats and tomato sauce), vegetables, cheeses (which may include ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan), and seasonings and spices, like Italian seasoning, such as garlic, oregano and basil.

The dish may be topped with grated cheese, which becomes melted after baking. Typically cooked pasta is assembled with the other ingredients and then baked in an oven. The resulting baked pasta is cut into single-serving square portions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne pasta sheets
  • Beef mince or alternative mince product
  • Canned tomatoes
  • 1 Tsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Large onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • 200ml beef or vegetable stock
  • 1 glass of red wine (optional)
  • 200ml milk
  • 50g flour
  • 50g butter
  • 200g grated cheese (whatever cheese is preferred)
  • Salt, pepper, oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat pan and add oil, make sure the pan is not too hot, gas mark 4 should be fine
  2. Chop onion and garlic roughly
  3. Add onion and garlic to pan and fry until onion is softened
  4. Add minced meat and fry until it starts to crackle, thats when you know the liquid has been cooked off
  5. Add tomato paste and mix well
  6. Add wine and beef stock, let the liquid reduce down then add can of tomatoes
  7. If too thick add some water and allow to simmer
  8. In a different pan create cheese sauce base, first by melting butter
  9. Add flour and cook well, incrementally add the milk and incorporate well with flour and butter mixture
  10. Once that has created a smooth sauce add the cheese, turn of heat and allow cheese to naturally incorporate into the sauce
  11. Use large deep oven proof dish,
    1. Place layer of pasta
    2. Place layer of tomato sauce
    3. Place layer of cheese sauce, in addition to a sprinkle of oregano on top of each cheese sauce layer
    4. Repeat these steps
  12. Finally place in the oven and cook on 180c fan oven for 45 minutes, once done allow to rest for 10 minutes before serving
