Kebab recipe

Description

a dish consisting of small pieces of meat, tomatoes, onions, etc, threaded onto skewers and grilled, generally over charcoalAlso called: shish kebab, kabob, cabob

Ingredients

  • Lamb mince
  • Cumin, coriander, paprika, chilli powder, salt, pepper
  • 1 egg
  • Pita bread
  • Choice of salad

Steps

  1. In a large bowl mix the lamb mince with the spices, ensuring the spices are combined well with the mince
  2. Using your hands, gently press the meat into a cylinder shape and place onto some tin foil
  3. Wrap the meat mixture in the foil nice and tightly, forming almost the shape of a burrito
  4. PLace in the oven on a low heat, 140c fan oven for 3-4 hours
  5. Once cooked remove from the foil and allow the meat to rest for 10-15 minutes
  6. Once rested, finely slice the meat, a serrated knife works best
  7. Now simply assemble your kebab, with choice of salad, sauces and bread
